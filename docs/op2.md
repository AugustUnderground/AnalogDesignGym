### Symmetrical Amplifier (OP2)

![op2](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/matthschw/ace/main/figures/op2.png)

Registered as `gace:op2-<tech>-<variant>`.

#### Observation Space

| Technology | Dimensions     |
|------------|----------------|
| `xh035`    | `ℝ²⁰⁶∈(-∞ ;∞)` |
| `sky130`   | `ℝ²⁶⁶∈(-∞ ;∞)` |
| `gpdk180`  | `ℝ³⁰⁶∈(-∞ ;∞)` |

```python
# xh035
gym.spaces.Box( low   = -np.inf
              , high  = np.inf
              , shape = (206 , )
              , dtype = np.float32
              , )

# sky130
gym.spaces.Box( low   = -np.inf
              , high  = np.inf
              , shape = (266 , )
              , dtype = np.float32
              , )

# gpdk180
gym.spaces.Box( low   = -np.inf
              , high  = np.inf
              , shape = (306 , )
              , dtype = np.float32
              , )
```
